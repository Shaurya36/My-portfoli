About The Event

Where Ideas Take Flight

TechNova 2025 is Silver Oak University's biggest annual technology extravaganza — a two-day fiesta packed with hackathons, expert talks, AI showcases, workshops, competitions and cultural nights.

Whether you're a coder, designer, entrepreneur, or just curious — TechNova has something for you. Come learn, compete, network, and create the future of technology.

⚡ Hackathon 🎤 Expert Talks 🤖 AI Showcase 💡 Startup Pitch 🏆 Competitions

What To Expect

Event Highlights

⚡

24-Hr Hackathon

Build, break, and ship real products. Teams of 2–4 compete for a ₹1.5L top prize.

🎤

Keynote Talks

Industry veterans and innovators share insights on AI, Web3, startups, and the future of tech.

🤖

AI Showcase

See live demos of student and startup AI projects — from chatbots to computer vision systems.

💡

Startup Pitch

Got an idea? Pitch it to real investors and mentors. Top pitches win seed funding and incubation support.

🔬

Hands-on Workshops

Learn Prompt Engineering, Cybersecurity, Full-Stack Dev, Robotics, and more in intensive workshops.

🎭

Cultural Night

End day one with an electrifying cultural night — music, performances, and networking under the stars.
